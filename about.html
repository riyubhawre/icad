Training

ICAD provides comprehensive package for students seeking admission for undergraduate programs (EXCEL-SAT), Graduate programs (EXCEL-GRE) and Management programs (EXCEL-GMAT) in United States of America & Canada.

  • EXCEL-GRE (GRE Training, Testing, Adrmission & VISA Asistance)
  • EXCEL-SAT (SAT Training, Testing. Admission & ViSA Assistance)
  • EXCEL-GMAT (GMAT Training, Testing, Admission & VISA Assistance)

    • Academics at ICAD

    With most experienced and expert ICAD Faculty, ICAD'S Training Session comprises of 12 weeks of Aptitude Grooming Training Sessions and 4 weeks of Testing Sessions. Training Session covers Vocabulary building, Comprehension, Quantitative and Analytical Writing skills development which is required to crack GRE/SAT/GMAT. Along with comprehensive training, students are provided with 1CAD's 12 updated publications for self practice. Testing session tests the students' preparation in simulated exam atmosphere with standard software, which matches standard marking system and content. ICAD is the only organization that developed the ICAD'S CRACK GRE testing software based on standard marking system and content.
